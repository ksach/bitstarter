FAQ

How will it work?
1. Download Beer Me for free.
2. Go to your favourite pub/bar/club
3. Press the big "BEER ME" button
4. Receive your beer
But really, how will it work?
When you press the big "BEER ME" button, the bartender in the pub you're in will be notified of your thirst instantly and will know where to find you via GPS and/or a QR code to be scanned at your table. Being eager to please, they will bring you your beer without delay.
What if my local pub does not describe to Beer Me?
If you press the big "BEER ME" button but your local pub has not yet subscribed, we will let you know that you're on your own for now. But never fear, we'll send a message to the pub urging them to subscribe so that you can use Beer Me the next time. The more you use it, the more likely they will subscribe.
